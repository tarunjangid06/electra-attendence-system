What We Offer

Everything You Need

A complete attendance management system built specifically for Nexa Kanakpura workshop.

📍
GPS Verification
Workers can only check in when they are physically within 100 meters of the workshop. No fake attendance.
📊
Personal Dashboard
Each worker has their own dashboard showing total days, monthly attendance, and full history.
🔐
Secure Login
Every worker has a unique username and password. Data is private and secure per account.
🛡️
Admin Panel
Admin can view all workers, filter by date, see who is present today, and review full summaries.
🕐
Check In & Out
Track exact check-in and check-out times for every worker every day automatically.
📋
Full History
All attendance records are saved permanently. View any worker's history from day one.
